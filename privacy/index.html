Introduction

We understand that you trust us with your information. The security of your privately held information holds our greatest attention. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you engage with our Luxury car, yacht, and chauffeur rental services in the UAE.

Information We Collect Personal Information

When you make a booking, or sending us an inquiry through our website we may need your full name, contact information, and payment details. We use it for your reservation and service that will meet your preferences.

usage data

We may also collect non-personal data such as your IP address, browser type, device used, and time spent on our site. This information helps us understand the behavior of users of our platform so as to improve on our offerings.

How We Use Your Information Booking & Service Fulfillment

Your personal data enables us to confirm bookings, send updates, and offer smooth coordination of services including vehicle delivery, yacht charter schedules, or chauffeur logistics.

Customer Experience

We may use the information to personalize your experience, understand preferences, and provide relevant suggestions or enhancements during future interactions.

Marketing Communication

If you choose, we may send you updates, promotional offers, or exclusive invites via email or SMS. You are free to unsubscribe at any time through the link provided in our messages.

Legal Compliance

Your data may be processed or shared when required or to comply with any legal obligations, respond to court orders or protect our rights, users, or business interests.

How We Protect Your Data

We apply industry-standard practices to protect your information from any unauthorized entry or misuse. For this encryption, secure servers and strict access controls are used. Only our authorized team members can handle your data. All our staff are trained on confidentiality and data protection protocols. While we do our utmost, no online transmission is 100% secure—so we cannot fully guarantee security.

Sharing With Third Parties

To fulfill your bookings, we may work with trusted third parties such as payment gateways, service providers and fleet partners. we have contractual obligation on these parties to keep your data safe and use it only for the intended purpose. We don't sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.
