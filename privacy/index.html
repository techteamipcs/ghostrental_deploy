Privacy Policy Ghost Rentals

Introduction

We, at Ghost Rentals, appreciate every customer’s trust in us in sharing their personal information. Therefore, privacy protection is very important to us. With this Privacy Policy, we explain how we will collect, use, and protect your data as you interact with our brand in renting luxury cars, yachts and chauffeurs in Dubai, UAE.

Information We Collect

personal information

When you book a service or send an inquiry through our website, we may collect:

  • Full Name
  • Contact Information (e.g., email, phone number)
  • Payment Details

This information is essential for processing your reservations and ensuring our services meet your preferences.

usage data

We also gather non-personal information, such as:

  • IP Address
  • Browser Type
  • Device Used
  • Time Spent on Our Website

This data helps us analyze user behavior and improve your experience on our platform.

How We Use Your Information

  1. Booking & Service Fulfillment

    Your personal data allows us to:

    • Confirm bookings
    • Send updates
    • Coordinate services like vehicle delivery, yacht charters, or chauffeur logistics
  2. Enhancing Customer Experience

    We use your information to personalize your experience, understand your preferences, and provide tailored recommendations for future interactions.

  3. Marketing Communication

    With your consent, we may send you:

    • Updates
    • Promotional offers
    • Exclusive invitations

    You can unsubscribe anytime using the link provided in our messages.

  4. Legal Compliance

    We may process or share your data to:

    • Comply with legal obligations
    • Respond to court orders
    • Protect our rights, users, or business interests

How We Protect Your Data

We implement industry-standard security measures to safeguard your information, including:

  • Encryption
  • Secure Servers
  • Strict Access Controls

Only authorized team members, trained in confidentiality and data protection, can access your data. While we strive to ensure the highest level of security, no online transmission is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services
  • Comply with legal requirements

If you wish to have your data deleted, we will honor your request unless it conflicts with legal or contractual obligations.

Sharing Your Information

  1. Trusted Third Parties

    To fulfill your bookings, we may share your data with trusted partners, such as:

    • Payment gateways
    • Service providers
    • Fleet partners

    These third parties are contractually obligated to keep your data secure and use it solely for the intended purpose.

  2. No Selling of Data

    We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review it regularly to stay informed about how we protect your data.

By using our services, you agree to the practices outlined in this policy.

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests related to your data, feel free to reach out to us:
